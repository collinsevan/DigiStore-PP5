Frequently Asked Questions

DigiStore sells digital products and downloads, including software, productivity tools, digital art, and audio books.

Digital products are purchased online and are intended for digital access or use. Product details on each listing help explain what is included before purchase.

You can browse products at any time. Creating an account is helpful because it gives you access to your profile and order history in one place.

If you have an account and have placed an order, you can view completed orders in your profile order history.

If you believe your completed order has not been received, please go to the support section below and contact us with your order reference and the email linked to your order so we can review it.

If you have a question about a product, a completed order, or a general store query, please use the contact details in the support section below.

Contact Support

Need help with an order or have a general question? Please reach out to us and include as much detail as possible so we can assist you.

For support with a completed order, please include:

  • Your order reference
  • The email address linked to the order
  • A short description of the issue

You can contact us at: support@digistore.com
